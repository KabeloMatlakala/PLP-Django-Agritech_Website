Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our marketplace

How do you ensure product quality?

We have a rigorous supplier verification process that includes business license verification, product certification review, facility inspection, and product sample testing. Additionally, we regularly conduct random product testing to ensure ongoing quality compliance. Our review system also helps maintain quality as suppliers with poor reviews are removed from our platform.

What are the shipping and delivery options?

We offer direct farm delivery for all products. Delivery timeframes vary by product type and location, but are typically 2-5 business days for most items. Bulk orders may take longer. You can track your delivery in real-time through our platform, and you'll receive notifications at each stage of the shipping process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payment options. For large orders, we also offer financing options through our agricultural lending partners. All payments are processed securely, and we never store your full credit card information on our servers.

Can I return products if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we have a satisfaction guarantee policy. If you're not satisfied with a product, you can return it within 14 days of delivery for a full refund. For seeds and perishable items, we offer replacements if germination rates or quality doesn't meet the specified standards. Please note that custom or specially ordered items may have different return policies.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, most products on our marketplace offer volume discounts for bulk purchases. The discount tiers vary by product and supplier, but are clearly displayed on each product page. Additionally, we offer seasonal promotions and group buying opportunities where farmers can pool their orders to access even better pricing.

How do I become a supplier on your marketplace?

To become a supplier, you need to complete our supplier application process, which includes providing business documentation, product certifications, and samples for testing. Our team will review your application and conduct necessary verifications. Once approved, you'll receive training on how to use our supplier portal to list products, manage inventory, and process orders.
