Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our crop monitoring system

What hardware do I need to get started?

To get started with our basic plan, you'll need at least one soil sensor per field and access to our mobile app. For more advanced monitoring, we recommend adding our weather station and considering our drone services for aerial imaging.

How accurate is the weather forecasting?

Our weather forecasting combines data from multiple sources, including the OpenWeatherMap API and our on-farm weather stations. This provides hyperlocal forecasts with over 90% accuracy for 3-day predictions and 80% accuracy for 7-day predictions.

Can the system work with my existing irrigation setup?

Yes, our system is designed to integrate with most modern irrigation systems. We provide API access in our Enterprise plan that allows for direct control of compatible irrigation systems based on soil moisture data.

How many sensors do I need per acre?

The number of sensors depends on your field's soil variability and crop type. As a general guideline, we recommend one soil sensor per 5-10 acres. Our team can help you determine the optimal sensor placement for your specific farm during setup.

Do I need technical expertise to use the system?

No technical expertise is required. Our system is designed to be user-friendly with an intuitive dashboard and mobile app. We also provide comprehensive onboarding and ongoing support to ensure you get the most out of our system.

What crops does the system support?

Our system supports a wide range of crops including grains (wheat, corn, rice), fruits, vegetables, nuts, and specialty crops. The AI models are continuously updated to improve detection and analysis for different crop types.
